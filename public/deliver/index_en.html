About Keypon Logistics

Keypon International Logistics (Guangzhou) Co., Ltd was founded in the Nansha Free Trade Zone in 2015—— a joint investment firm of Patent International Logistics (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd and UC Express Logistics Co., Ltd.

We devote to provide our business partners optimal cross-border logistics solutions, including bonded warehousing, customs clearance, pick & pack service to the door delivery in every corner of the country. Now, we focus on the services of B2B2C, B2C and C2C. We have met this challenge and guarantee the 100% realisation of your requirements.

At present, we manage more than 100,000 square feet of bonded warehouse, which adopts an advanced WMS management system and directly connecting with the customs system and the national inspection system. We can handle 20,000 orders per day.
